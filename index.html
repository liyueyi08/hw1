Hobbies

Momoe loves boxes and paper bags. Her favorate time is when we came back from shopping. However, because she love to use her mouth to explore the world, we have to throw them away instantly.

She is also excited about everything that is new. When we are with her, she is very curious and excited to charge into unknown spaces. But if there is another living thing, she would immediately run back home.

Fun Facts

Momoe is actually named after a well-known Japanese singer. Momoe Yamaguchi

Her nickname is Pronounced "new new", which is a common girl name. Chinese people think a common nickname would bring luck to children.
